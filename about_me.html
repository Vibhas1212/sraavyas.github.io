Q. Who am I?
A.  I am Sraavya, a 12 year old young girl who loves to read books!

Q. What is my height?
A.  I am 5’6. Which is not good, but still people tell me that I am really tall. I guess it comes from my       parents….

Q. What do I like to do in my free time?
A.  I would be free in my free time!

Q. What do I like to eat?
A.  I am a total Delhiite in the field of eating! I love Chole Bhature, Pani Puri, Aloo Parantha       and all kinds of Chaat!

Q. What do I like to read?
A.  I love to read fictitious storybooks like the all-time favourite series, Harry Potter, Keeper of the       Lost Cities, Artemis Fowl, The Unwanteds, The Percy Jackson series and many others!

Q. Who is my role model?
A.  My all-time role model is the great author JK Rowling!

Q. What is your biggest fear?
A.  I am terrified of lizards! I am also scared of moths, caterpillars, wasps, honeybees and all kinds of       insects.......

Q. What is your favourite movie?
A.  I don’t have a favourite movie per se, because I like all kinds of movies that I watch.

Q. What is your favourite series?
A.  My favourite series is Greenhouse Academy, streaming on Netflix right now. I thought that 4       seasons were not enough for such an amazing storyline!

Q. What is your favourite colour?
A.  I think any person who knows me very well can answer this. My favourite colour is teal. Derived       from the eye colour of Fitz.

Q. What is your favourite subject?
A.  Many people may judge me on this because students are not exactly favourable of this subject but my       favourite subject is History!

Q. What is your biggest pet peeve?
A.  My biggest pet peeve is when someone does not use proper punctuation and also when a person       boasts very much. I think that modesty is the best policy.

Q. Who is your favourite singer?
A.  My favourite singers are Olivia Rodrigo, Taylor Swift, Madison Hayes, Joshua Bassett and       Naomi Scott.

Q. What’s one monument you would want to visit?

Q. What is the thing that you hate?

Q. What do you love the most?
A.  Books… All kinds of books.
